Welcome to Rodent!

Rodent is a rat sighting tracking suite for use by citizens and governments - citizens report any rat sightings, and governments use this data in order to better coordinate extermination efforts. Rodent is available for public access, all you need to do is register, and you can start reporting those pesky rats!

Get started now »

Easy to Use

Rodent is surprisingly easy to use - it features just three major interfaces. You can add and list Rat Sightings easily, and it's very simple to look up statistics based on date.

Multi-platform

Rodent is available not only on the web, but as an iPhone and Android app too! As a result, it's easier than ever to report those little creatures.
